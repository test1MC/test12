Mac OS X 10.9 (Mavericks) and 10.10 (Yosemite)

Installing via CD

  1. Place installation CD into drive.
  2. If installation does not start immediately, browse to the CD and double-click the file.
  3. Click to accept the license agreement.
  4. To install, drag the icon into the Applications folder (below).

Installing via download

  1. Go to your Mac® System Preferences and click to open the Security & Privacy window.
  2. Click the lock in the bottom left corner of the window to allow editing, if necessary.
  3. Under the , click Mac App Store and identified developers under the Allow applications downloaded from option.
  4. Download, and double-click the file on your computer.
  5. Click to accept the license agreement.
  6. To install, drag the icon into the Applications folder.
