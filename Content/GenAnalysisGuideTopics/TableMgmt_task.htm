Columns can be re-ordered, sorted, filtered, and new columns can be added:

  • Click and drag a to move the column's position within the table.
  • Click a to quickly sort the column alphabetically or numerically (clicking repeatedly toggles the sort from ascending to descending).
  • Right click a to view more options.
  • Click above and to the right of the table to choose additional columns that can be added to the table.

For more details on how to use these tables, visit the Help System by clicking the blue question mark in the upper right corner of the window.
