HKP Quantification (800 nm image)

Use the Add Rectangle tool in Image Studio to quantify the HKP bands (800 nm channel). Loading indicator bands will also be analyzed in the 800 nm image.

Target Protein Quantification (800 nm image)

Use the Add Rectangle tool in to quantify the target protein band in each lane (800 nm channel).

  1. Add shapes to bands.

    1. In the Shape group on the Analysis tab, click Add Rectangle.

    2. Click each band to be analyzed, and an appropriately sized shape will be added around the band.

  2. Export the quantification data for your HKP and loading indicator target.

    1. Click Shapes to open the Shapes data table.

    2. Select shape data, then copy and paste data into a spreadsheet.

      All data fields will be exported, but “Signal” is the field of interest for analysis.
