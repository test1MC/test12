Comb Group

When an is applied to an image, lanes are created in a Lane Location Rectangle using the MPX comb selection.

To change the lanes, use the controls in the to redraw the boundary or change the comb selection.

If there are already lanes defined on the current analysis, redrawing the boundary deletes those lanes. Nothing is preserved from the deleted lanes such as lane position or edited bands.

To redraw the boundary:

  1. Click the .
  2. Click the upper left corner of the area to define and drag the boundary box to the bottom right of the area to define, then release the mouse button.
  3. After the lanes are defined, you can manually fine tune them by moving the lanes. Click the lane to select it, then drag the top or the bottom to tilt it or hold down the shift key to move the whole lane left or right to a new position. To select more than one lane to move or tilt, hold down the key while clicking (selecting) the lanes. Move the cursor to the top or bottom of the lane and when the cursor turns to the four-pointed arrow, drag it left or right to tilt the group; or hold down the key, drag the cursor left or right to move the group. (The process also optimizes the lane location.)

After band finding is complete, if you move or resize a lane, the band-finding process is repeated using the same parameters.
