Colorized TIFF Files

Use this method to export a color image without annotations. This method will export the current color view of the image, whether it is 700 channel alone, 800 channel alone, or both channels overlaid.

Procedure:

  1. Click > > Colorize Tif Files. The dialog will open.

  2. In the dialog, navigate to a folder where the image will be saved and name the image.
  3. Click to export the image.

Clicking > Hide Annotations and then using and choosing the format will accomplish the same export as .
