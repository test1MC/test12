Position the Plate

  1. Ensure the instrument's plate carrier is clean, dry, and free of any residue or dust.

  2. Place the in the plate carrier with the text facing up and the alignment grid aligned with the lower left corner of the plate carrier.

    For the second scan, rotate the plate 180 degrees.

Begin Scan

  2. Connect to the instrument.

  3. On the Acquire tab, ensure the following settings are chosen.

    Setup group: Choose the Plate preset.

    Channels group: Set intensity for both channels to 6.

    Scan Controls group: Set the Resolution to 100 µm. Set the Focus Offset to 2.2 mm by using the Custom Focus Offset option at the bottom of the Focus Offset menu.

  5. Perform a second scan with the same settings, but with the plate in Scan Position 2.

  6. Rotate the Scan Position 2 image to the same orientation as the first image.

    On the Image tab, click > Rotate 180°.

    Note: This process creates a new image. Use the new, rotated image for analysis.
