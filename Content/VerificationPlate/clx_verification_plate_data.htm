Analyze Odyssey CLx Verification Plate Data ()

Copy data from verification plate () scans into the workbook provided below to quickly calculate 700 and 800 channel coefficients of variation (CV).

Prepare data and export it from Image Studio Download the workbook and analyze data

  1. Ensure the data columns in the spreadsheet you exported from Image Studio are in the following order (as described above).

  2. Download the workbook.

  3. Copy data from the spreadsheet you exported from Image Studio into the Data sheet of the workbook provided in step 2.

    If "No" appears in the Column Name Match section of the Calculations sheet, the wrong columns are included or the columns are in the wrong order.

The CVs for well-to-well variation will be calculated and displayed in the Results sheet.

Interpret the Results

Coefficients of variation are displayed in the Results sheet. The maximum variation for all locations should be less than 5% for the 700 and 800 channel. The appropriate table cells in the Results sheet will be highlight red if CVs exceed 5%.

If you notice greater than 5% variability, email regulatory@licor.com.
