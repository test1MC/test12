Recertify the Verification Plate

The Odyssey does not automatically come with the purchase of an instrument. More information about s is available on the regulatory products page.

The Odyssey should be sent to LI-COR annually for recertification. Contact biosupport@licor.com to request the service (service number ).

During the recertification process, the will be compared to a standard. The plate will be cleaned and/or repaired as necessary. You will receive a certificate of verification that shows your plate's variation from the standard.
