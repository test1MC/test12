Spot Layout Group

  1. Adjust the number of rows and columns in the arrays by entering new values or using the up and down arrows to incrementally increase or decrease the number of rows or columns. All of the arrays are changed.
  2. Use the and buttons to incrementally move the spots in the arrays.
  3. Click the small arrow at the right edge of the to open the where you can set the spot size, spacing between the spots in the arrays, and how the spots are named. There is also a grid where you can define each spot as either Signal, Background, or None.
