HKP Validation Procedure

You must run at least two separate test blots to ensure your HKP meets the requirements for normalization.

Test blot 1: Run a test blot with a panel of candidate HKPs to find a HKP that is unaffected by the conditions of your experiment. See Step 1 on page 1.

Test blot 2: Run another blot to verify that the HKP candidate you selected based on the first blot can be detected in the same linear range as your target. See Step 1 on page 1.

Always remember to document your validation procedure and results.
