Total Protein Stain Normalization Unaffected by Biological Variation

When normalizing with , you do not need to perform any validation for Requirement 1.

Total protein staining measures the aggregate protein signal in each lane, and eliminates the error that can be introduced by using a single internal control protein (2, 4, 13, 14, 15). Rather than using a single internal control protein as a surrogate marker for sample concentration, total protein staining directly compares the aggregate sample protein in each lane.
