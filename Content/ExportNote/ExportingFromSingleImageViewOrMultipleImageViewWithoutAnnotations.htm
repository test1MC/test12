Exporting from Single Image View or Multiple Image View Without Annotations

Images exported using the or will include all displayed annotations.

To prevent annotations from appearing in the exported image(s), do one of the following:

  • Export some annotations but not others: Find the in the and disable the box next to any annotation type that should not appear in the exported image.
  • Turn off all annotations: Click the button at the top of the located at the right side of the window to hide all annotations (for version 4.0 or later).
