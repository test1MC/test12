Questions to Answer During In Vivo Validation

Questions that should be addressed prior to using your labeled targeting agent in an animal study include:

  • Is the labeled targeting agent stable?

  • Does it find the target cell?

  • What areas, other than the intended target, show retention of the probe?

  • What is the route of elimination?

  • How long does it take to clear the target and how long does it take to clear the animal?

  • Will the clearance time affect your ability to detect the target of interest?

  • What dose is required for adequate target detection?
