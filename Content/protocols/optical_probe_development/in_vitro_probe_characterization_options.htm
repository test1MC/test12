In Vitro Characterization

A variety of assays can be used to determine selectivity of your labeled targeting agent, including In-Cell Westerns and On-Cell Westerns.

  • The In-Cell Western is a microplate-based immunofluorescence assay used to detect proteins in permeabilized cells, i.e., a relevant cellular context.
  • On-Cell Westerns are a receptor-based binding assay and may include dose response and competitive binding evaluations.

These high throughput methods (96- or 384-well microtiter plates) provide a quick and effective way to understand labeled targeting agent activity toward the intended target.
