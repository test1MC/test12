Introduction to Ex Vivo Imaging

Imaging organs and tissues with the Pearl Trilogy is intuitive and straightforward.

Once a signal has been identified non-invasively, the next questions that need to be addressed include:

  • Are there specific organs of retention?
  • Are signals uniform or mottled within an organ?

The Pearl Trilogy Organ Tray Base and Disposable Organ Trays were designed specifically for excised organ and whole tissue evaluations after administration of labeled targeting agents.

Illumination on the Pearl Trilogy imaging bed is even, ensuring that multiple organs can be imaged together under uniform light exposure. You can then make a rapid visual assessment for multiple organs from an individual animal or for a specific organ from multiple animals.

After imaging, the Small Animal Image analysis key provides a fast, intuitive way to quantify signal.
