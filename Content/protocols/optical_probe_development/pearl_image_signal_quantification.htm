Signal Quantification

A standard set of image analysis tools is available in Image Studio, and the Small Animal Image Analysis key can be installed to provide functionality specifically for analyzing animal images.

  • Regions of interest: You can define regions of interest (ROI), i.e. areas of the image with signal to be quantified, using rectangles, ellipses, or a shape drawn freehand.
  • Image editing: You can cut, crop, copy, paste, delete, duplicate, and rotate images, as you would expect from software named “Image Studio”.
  • Small Animal Image (SAI) analysis: The SAI key significantly reduces analysis time by allowing you to define and quantify ROI quickly. After choosing a couple parameters and setting a background shape, you can click the center of an ROI and Image Studio will automatically draw a shape around the ROI.
