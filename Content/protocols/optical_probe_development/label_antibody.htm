Label an Antibody

Labeling an antibody is relatively straightforward, and several labeling kits are commercially available. Chemistries commonly used to label antibodies include:

  • N-hydroxysuccinimidyl (NHS) ester labeling of primary amine (lysine residues)
  • Sulfhydryl labeling of cysteine residues

Labeled antibody targeting agents can be purified using size exclusion spin columns.
