In-Cell Western and On-Cell Western Image Analysis

The In-cell Western key for Odyssey CLx should be installed which allows you to apply a template to the microtiter plate image. With a few plate-setup details, Image Studio can do the ICW % Response calculations rapidly. Inputs include: 1) apply microtiter plate template, 2) adjust ROIs to better fit the microtiter plate image. 3) identify the normalization channel (800nm or 700nm) and 4) specific well identification (Backgrounds, Controls, or Samples).

The In-Cell Western analysis is commonly used to find the following values:

  • EC50
  • IC50
  • Kd
  • Ki
