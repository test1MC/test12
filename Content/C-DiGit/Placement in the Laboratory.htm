Placement in the Laboratory

The C‑DiGit Blot Scanner weighs approximately 2.18 kg (4.8 lbs.). The instrument should be placed on a level surface that is at a workable height for the user.

Ambient Laboratory Conditions

Place the C‑DiGit Blot Scanner away from external heat sources (furnaces, windows, etc.). Additional heating can cause high temperatures within the enclosure. Place the instrument away from sinks or other sources of water that pose a shock hazard. Recommended operating conditions are 15-30 °C and a dew point no greater than 25 °C to prevent condensation on the instrument.

Space Requirements

The C‑DiGit Blot Scanner requires an area 27.94 cm L (11.0") x 22.23 cm W (8.75") x 7.3 cm H (2.875", lid closed), or 31.12 cm, (12.25”, lid open).
