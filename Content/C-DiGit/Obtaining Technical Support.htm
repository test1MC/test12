Obtaining Technical Support

To resolve a problem with your C‑DiGit Blot Scanner, start by contacting LI‑COR Technical Support at , or sending an e-mail to biohelp@licor.com. Outside of the U.S., contact your local sales office or distributor. Be prepared to give the serial number of your instrument, which can be found on a label on the instrument underside.

LI‑COR Technical Support may ask you to provide copies of the instrument log files to aid in diagnosing problems.
