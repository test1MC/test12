What’s in the Box

In addition to the C‑DiGit Blot Scanner, you will find the items shown below Use only the provided cables with the C‑DiGit Blot Scanner. (l-r): 1) Ethernet and 2) USB cables, 3) a power supply with interchangeable plugs, 4) a C‑DiGit Target image, and 5) Image Studio and Resource CDs.

The C‑DiGit Target is a photosensitive card used to verify that your C‑DiGit Blot Scanner is functioning properly; the card is briefly exposed to room light and then imaged on the instrument. If the acquired pattern matches the target image, the C‑DiGit Blot Scanner is functioning as it should, and is ready to image your sample. Instructions for using the Target are given below in the Quick Start Tutorial on page 1. The Target should be stored in the blue envelope when not in use.
