Washing Buffer

  1. Does it matter how I wash the membranes after antibody incubation?
  2. Yes. Adequately washing the membranes will greatly improve the appearance of the chemiluminescent Western blot. Wash the membranes with a saline- buffered solution containing 0.05 to 0.1% of a non-ionic detergent such as Tween® 20. Wash four times for five minutes each on a shaker or rotator with ample wash solution.
