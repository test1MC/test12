Set the Lanes

Lanes are represented by vertical lines that transect bands in a lane.

Lanes must be selected in the of the for the lanes to be visible.

The correct number of lanes must be designated in the . Lanes only need to be positioned close to the center of a band, because the lanes will be optimally positioned by the software during band finding.

To adjust a lane's position:

  1. Click the lane to select it (selected lanes are dashed lines).

  2. Click and drag the anchor point (dashed square) at the top or bottom of the lane to move only the top or bottom end point of the lane.

    Hold while moving an anchor to shift the entire lane.

  3. Click anywhere away from the lane to set it.

Repeat this procedure until you are satisfied that the lanes are positioned close to the center of the bands. Lanes will be positioned optimally during band finding.
