Re-size or Move a Band

Bands can be re-sized or moved (if only one channel is visible).

    • Move a band by placing the cursor inside the shape. The cursor will become a that can be used to move the band.

      The band cannot be moved away from the lane it is in.

    • a band by pointing the cursor to the midpoint of a side of the box or to the corner of the box. A double-headed arrow will appear that can be used to adjust the size of the box.
  2. Set the changes by clicking outside the shape.
