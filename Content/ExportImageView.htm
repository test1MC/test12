Export Image View: Exporting Image with Annotations

Use this method to export the image with its annotations.

Procedure:

  1. Click > > . The window will open.
  2. In the window, select the desired image format and resolution.
  3. Click . The dialog box will open.
  4. In the dialog, navigate to the folder where the exported file will be saved and name the image.
  5. Click to export the file.
