Export Options

Click the to open the , then point to to view the export options. The following can be exported from :

  • Images: Images can be exported individually, in batches of individual files, or multiple images can be exported to a single composite image. Exported images can be in , , or format at 150, 300, or 600 dots per inch (dpi) resolution. During export, images can be resized and the chosen resolution will remain the same after resizing.

  • Color Bar: The color bar from an image can be exported by itself to compare images in a presentation, publication, or lab notebook.
  • Movies: Export a QuickTime movie or a batch of individual images to be made into a movie using third party software.
  • Image Studio Files: Export containing images, scan information, and analysis data to or to another . Use this option to backup or archive data.
