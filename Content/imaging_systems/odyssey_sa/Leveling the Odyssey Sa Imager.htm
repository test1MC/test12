Leveling the Odyssey Sa Imager

  1. If necessary, move the BioStack Plate Stacker so there is at least 18 cm (7 in.) clearance between the Odyssey Sa Imager and the BioStack plate gripper. (If there is insufficient clearance, the extended plate carrier will collide with the BioStack accessory.)
  2. Push the eject button on the front panel to extend the plate carrier. Place the leveling and alignment fixture into the plate carrier as shown below.
  4. Note: If the bubble level is difficult to see, it can be moved to the alternate position as shown above.
  5. Turn the leveling feet in or out on the until the bubble falls within the circle on the bubble level, indicating that the instrument is level.
  6. Important: Do not extend the legs over 3 cm (1.2 in.). The instrument may become unstable or potentially damage the enclosure. If necessary, turn the legs back in as you level the instrument rather than continuing to extend the legs.
  7. Press downward on each of the four corners of the to make sure the instrument does not rock and that all four legs are contacting the surface. If necessary, adjust any legs as needed and level the instrument again.
  8. Remove the leveling and alignment fixture from the plate carrier and push the eject button to retract the plate carrier.
