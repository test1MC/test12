Installing the Locator Clamp

A locator clamp is provided that locks around the front legs of the . In later steps, the BioStack stand will be connected to the locator clamp, which locks both the Odyssey Sa and the BioStack Plate Stacker in position.

  1. Make sure the clamp is open before starting. Flip the latch cover up and rotate the latch until it disengages from the hook, if necessary.
  2. Place the clamp on the surface in front of the with the jaws open. Slide the left side of the clamp under the instrument in such a way that the left-front leg passes between the jaws of the clamp.
  3. Push the right side of the clamp under the instrument and move the clamp to the right so the right-front leg passes between the jaws of the clamp. When properly positioned, the legs of the will rest in the indentations in the front of the clamp.
  4. Close the clamp by placing the latch over the hook and turning the latch. After the latch is rotated, it can be pressed down into its locked position.
