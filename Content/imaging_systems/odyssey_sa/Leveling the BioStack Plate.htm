Leveling the BioStack™ Plate Stacker

  1. Push the eject button on the front panel to extend the plate carrier.
  2. Move the BioStack Plate Stacker directly in front of the . Be careful not to strike the plate carrier.
  3. Look between the instruments with your line of sight level with the plate carrier. Adjust the leveling feet on the BioStack stand so the plate carrier is about 2 mm below the linear bearing on the BioStack Plate Stacker.
  5. Important: Be careful during the height adjustments. Do not let the plate carrier collide with the linear bearing on the BioStack Plate Stacker.
  6. Push the eject button on the front panel to retract the plate carrier.
  7. Turn off the BioStack Plate Stacker.
  8. Slide the leveling and alignment fixture into the BioStack™ plate gripper and make sure it rests flat on the gripper arms. The “A1” corner of the fixture should be in the same orientation that it was in the plate carrier. Push the fixture all the way in until it contacts the gripper arms.
  10. Adjust the leveling feet on the BioStack stand until the bubble level indicates the plate gripper is level. Adjust the feet only enough to level the instrument, while trying to maintain the same height and clearance from the plate carrier.
  11. Move the plate gripper to its highest position and press the eject button on the to extend the plate carrier. Verify that the clearance between the BioStack linear bearing and the Odyssey Sa plate carrier is still about 2 mm. Adjust the height and level the BioStack Plate Stacker again as needed.
  12. Push the eject button to retract the plate carrier. Remove the leveling and alignment fixture from the plate gripper.
