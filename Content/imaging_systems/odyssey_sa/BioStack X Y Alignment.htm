BioStack X-Y Alignment

The BioStack Plate Stacker is now close to the final position, but some small movements will be needed in the X-Y dimensions to ensure that microplates are placed squarely on the plate carrier.

  1. With the leveling and alignment fixture still in the BioStack plate gripper, manually lower the plate gripper until it is just above the plate carrier. (Make sure the BioStack stacker is off.)
  2. Observe the position of the fixture relative to the four alignment pins on the plate carrier. When the X-Y alignment is correct, the fixture will fit as shown below.
  4. If you observe that the fixture will contact one or more of the pins as it is lowered, carefully move the BioStack™ Plate Stacker in small increments until the fixture drops onto the surface of the plate carrier without hitting any of the pins and binding.
  5. Important: Move the BioStack Plate Stacker carefully and only in small increments. Excessive movement can stress the plate gripper assembly, potentially causing damage.
  6. Observe the bubble level on the fixture and make any necessary adjustments to level the BioStack Plate Stacker.
  7. Tighten the knob on both alignment pins. You may need to hold the threaded washer until the knob begins to tighten.
  9. Manually raise the BioStack plate gripper and remove the leveling and alignment fixture. Use the fifth thumbscrew to secure the fixture to the BioStack stand for storage.
  11. Press the eject button on the to retract the plate carrier.
  12. Turn on the BioStack instrument and wait for it to initialize.
