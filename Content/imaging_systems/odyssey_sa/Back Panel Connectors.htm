Back Panel Connectors

Below the fan on the back panel are a variety of connectors for the server hardware inside the instrument. None of these connectors should be used except the network port. The network port is used to connect the Odyssey Sa instrument to the computer via a Cat. 5 RJ45 crossover cable (Assembling the Odyssey Sa System on page 1).

The receptacle for the power cable is also located on the back panel.

Note: The power switch for the power supply on the back panel should always be in the ON position. For normal operation, the instrument should always be powered on and off from the power switch on the instrument front panel.
