Obtaining Technical Support

To resolve a problem with your Pearl Imager, start by contacting LI‑COR Technical Support at 800-645-4260, or e-mailing biohelp@licor.com. Be prepared to give the serial number of your instrument, which can be found on a label near the gas inlet/outlet ports on the right side of the instrument.

. Pearl Imager serial number label.

LI‑COR Technical Support may ask you to provide copies of the instrument log files to aid in diagnosing problems.
