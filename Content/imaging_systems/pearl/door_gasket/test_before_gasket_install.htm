Test for Light Leakage Before Installing Gasket

  1. Verify that the passes the "Verify Normal Operations Test" (VNOT), and leave the target in the .

    The VNOT procedure is available here licor.com/TrilogyVNOT.

    If your instrument does not pass the VNOT, please contact Technical Support at licor.com/biotechsupport.

  2. Ensure that the normal ambient lights are on (e.g. overhead lights or desk lights).

  3. Image the target in the BLI channel only, and examine the image to determine if there is any light leakage.
