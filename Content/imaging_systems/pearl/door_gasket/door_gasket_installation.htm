Door Gasket Installation

  1. Carefully slide the applicator tool and gasket out of the open side of the bag.

  2. Check the entire length of the gasket to ensure it is properly seated in the groove of the applicator along the entire length.

    If necessary, you can use a small tool or your fingernails to press the gasket into the proper position.

  3. Open the drawer.

  4. Remove the imaging bed.

  5. Turn the off.

  6. Place the square piece of foam under the door (as shown below) to hold the door up.

  7. Grab a corner of the tab on the red adhesive covering and begin carefully pulling off the adhesive covering.

    Ensure the gasket stays inside the applicator as you remove the adhesive covering.

    If you need to push the gasket back into the applicator, be careful to push the edge of the gasket without touching the adhesive.

  8. Hold the applicator tool near the top edge of the door with the gasket facing the inside surface of the door.

  9. Place the applicator just below the upper edge of the door with the top of the applicator touching the top edge of the door.

    Center the gasket while holding the applicator against the inside surface of the door.

  10. Push the applicator firmly against the inside surface of the door.

  11. Firmly pinch the applicator against the door to seat the gasket.

    Remove the applicator, leaving the gasket adhered to the door.

  12. Examine the placement of the gasket. It should be straight across the top of the door.

  13. Use your fingers to firmly seat the gasket all the way across from left to right.

  14. Remove the foam cube that is holding up the door.
