Do Not Use DMSO or Other Strong Solvents

CAUTION: Avoid contacting the imaging bed with strong solvents, including dimethyl sulfoxide (DMSO). Permanent discoloration of the imaging bed surface will result. In addition, any products in DMSO will incorporate into the surface of the imaging bed and will be visible in images.

ATTENTION: Les dissolvants forts, y compris DMSO, décoloreront de manière permanente la surface du lit d'image. Évitez tout contact. En outre, tous les produits d'IRDye dans DMSO incorporeront à la surface du lit et seront évidents dans les  images.

Whenever possible, inject the animal on a separate surgical bed and clean off any exterior dye product on the animal before transfer to the Pearl Imaging Bed.

Assembly and Installation

Press a nose cone onto its black base and insert a nose cone plug.

For imaging bed installation, see the Pearl Operator’s Manual.

Imaging Bed Spare Parts

The following parts should be included with your Pearl Imaging Bed.

  • Replacement Nose Cones: Replaces unsanitary or damaged nose cones.

  • Nose Cone Plugs: Used to plug nose cones and prevent leaks while anesthesia gas is flowing, but a mouse is not yet inserted in the nose cone.
  • O-rings and Neoprene Cord: Used to restrain the mouse during imaging.
  • Heater Plate Contact Pins: Used to replace bent or damaged contact pins on the underside of the imaging bed.

See your Operator's Manual for more information about the parts listed above.
