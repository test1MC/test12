Computer Connections and Networking

The computer supplied with the has two Ethernet ports. During installation, the port labeled LAN1 on the Pearl Imaging System control module (Figure 1‑3) must be connected to one of the Ethernet ports on the computer. On instruments with a serial number below 3100, the must be connected to the Ethernet port that is directly connected to the computer's motherboard (see Figure 1‑1).

Note: Use only the supplied Cat. 5e RJ45 Ethernet cable to connect the Pearl Imaging System and computer.

. Ethernet connector on the computer motherboard of a typical computer (other models will vary).

When starts, it searches for and discovers any Pearl Imagers on the network. If only one instrument is found, establishes communication. If more than one instrument is found, you will be presented with a list of instruments and asked which to use.
