By contrast, tissue penetration depths are only a few millimeters in the visible region of the spectrum where autofluorescence and light scattering are comparatively high. Above 900 nm, water absorption can interfere with signal-to-background ratio. 680RD and IRDye 800CW (particularly IRDye 800CW) are not hindered by interfering autofluorescence and yield high signal-to-background, making them ideally suited for optical imaging in small animals.
