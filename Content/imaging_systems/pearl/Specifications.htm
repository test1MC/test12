Specifications

Operating Conditions: For indoor use only; operating temperature 15-35 °C and dew point < 22 °C, non-condensing; maximum operating temperature may be reduced at elevations above 2000m.

Environmental Conditions: Pollution Degree 2.

Detector Type: CCD. Thermoelectrically cooled.

Acquisition Time: 500 ms to 40 s.

Acquisition Time: 500 ms to 60 s.

Wavelength Maxima for Excitation/Emission Bands:

Channel Excitation Emission
700 Channel 685 nm 720 nm
800 Channel 785 nm 820 nm
White Channel White 720 nm
Bioluminescence NA 450 - 650 nm

Dyes: Optimized for 680RD and IRDye 800CW.

Resolution: 85, 170, and 255 micron.

Image Display Options: Pseudocolor, grayscale, single color (red, green, or blue), or two colors with overlapping fluorescence displayed in a third color.

Capacity: One animal, with linked look-up tables for accurate multiple image comparisons.

Imaging Drawer: 16.8 cm W x 12 cm D (6.6” W x 4.75” D). Vertical clearance to top of drawer is 3.8 cm (1.5”).

Field of View: 11.2 cm W x 8.4 cm D (4.4” W x 3.3” D) at the surface of the imaging bed.

Imaging Drawer Temperature Range: 32-42 °C.

Gas Anesthesia: Inlet and outlet ports flow anesthesia gas through a nose cone in the imaging drawer. A rotameter is included for controlling flow rate. Recommended anesthesia system: EZ-2000 system (Euthanex Corporation, www.ezanesthesia.com).

Power Requirements: Universal input range is between 100-127 VAC and 200-240 VAC (voltage fluctuations not to exceed 10% of the nominal voltage); 50/60 Hz; at 120V, 4 Amp maximum; at 120V, 0.3 Amp typical when idle. Overvoltage Category II.

Computer Connection Cable: Cat. 5e RJ45, 10BASE-T/100BASE-TX/1000BASE-T. Use only the supplied cable.

Size: 41 W x 41 D x 66 cm H (16” W x 16” D x 26” H). Depth with imaging drawer open is 63.5 cm (25”).

Weight: 25 kg (55 lb).

The Pearl Imager is certified as a Class 1 laser product. Hazardous laser radiation is not emitted outside the instrument. Radiation emitted inside the Pearl Imager is confined within protective housings and external covers. A series of safety interlocks ensures that laser beams cannot escape during any phase of user operation.
