Stopping the Anesthesia Gas Flow

Anesthesia gas flow to the Pearl Imager can be deliberately stopped in one of three ways;

  1. Disconnecting the external supply from the Pearl instrument,
  2. Closing the adjustment valve on the rotameter,
  3. Removing the imaging bed or raising the imaging bed handle to the vertical position.

In all cases, the external anesthesia system should be properly configured with a drain or relief (such as an induction chamber) so as not to harm the user or anesthesia equipment with the buildup of pressure.
