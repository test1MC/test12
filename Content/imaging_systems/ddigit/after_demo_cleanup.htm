Clean D-DiGit After Demo

Appropriate Personal Protective Equipment, especially gloves, should be worn for this procedure.

To avoid contamination, fresh wipes should be used to clean each section of the scanner (as described below).

Turn off the scanner and unplug the power cable from the AC outlet.

Wipe the top and sides

  1. Wipe the gray plastic top and sides of the scanner with a Clorox Disinfecting Wipe (#869-10013).

  2. Wipe dry with a Kimwipe.

  3. Moisten a large Kimwipe with warm water and use it to wipe down the top and sides of the scanner.

  4. Wipe dry with a Kimwipe.

Wipe around the scan surface and under the handle

  1. With a new Clorox Disinfecting Wipe, wipe the plastic around the scan surface and under the handle.

    Do not clean the foam under the lid with Clorox wipes, as this may cause degradation and discoloration.

  2. Wipe dry with a Kimwipe.

  3. Moisten a large Kimwipe with warm water and use it to wipe the plastic around the scan surface and under the handle.

  4. Wipe dry with a Kimwipe.

Wipe the scan surface with deionized water

  1. Moisten a large Kimwipe with deionized water and wipe the scan surface.

  2. Clean the scan surface at least twice.

  3. Wipe the glass dry with a Kimwipe.

    If some residue remains on the glass, try washing with a small amount of methanol on a Kimwipe. Ensure that methanol does not get on the foam piece under the lid.

Perform the Verification of Normal Operation Test before any further use of the instrument.
