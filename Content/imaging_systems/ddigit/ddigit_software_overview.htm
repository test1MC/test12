  2. Exposure Time: Drag the slider to adjust exposure time.

  4. Light: Click to activate the blue LED for gel excision or observation.

  5. Settings: Click to open the Settings window where you can adjust scan settings, image file settings, and storage settings.

  6. Crop: Crop the selected image.

  7. Print: Print the selected image.
