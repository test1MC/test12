Scan Gel

The scanner must be unlocked before scanning. See Unlock Scanner on page 1 for instructions.

  1. Start .

  2. Click Image Capture on the Main Control Panel to image the gel.

    Tip: The default exposure time is 4x. For higher sensitivity, drag the slider bar to the right to increase the exposure time. Drag the slider bar to the left to decrease the exposure time and lower sensitivity.

After a scan is completed, the scanned image will be saved to the folder defined under Settings . For information about changing the folder location, see Modify Settings on page 1.
