Packaging Procedure

Please package the scanner according to these instructions before shipping it back to LI-COR.

Ensure the scanner is locked before continuing.

Do not return the SYBR® Safe Stain to LI-COR.

Locate items to be shipped

  • 1

  • 1 Power cord

  • 1 Power adapter

  • 1 Viewing shield

  • 1 USB cable

  • 1 Large cardboard box

  • 1 Cardboard sheet bordered with foam

  • 1 Cardboard sheet without foam

  • 2 Foam pieces

  • 1 Large plastic bag for scanner

    1 Medium plastic bag for Viewing shield

  • 3 Small plastic bags (for cords)

    Bags of desiccant

Wrap scanner in bag

Wrap the scanner and desiccant in the large plastic bag.

Place bottom foam piece in box

The bottom foam piece is the piece with semicircular cuts on the outer edge. Place the bottom foam piece in the box in the orientation shown.

Place the scanner in the box

Place the scanner on its side into the bottom foam piece with the top of the scanner toward the back of the box. The plastic feet on the bottom of the scanner should fit into grooves in the bottom foam piece.

Place the top foam piece on the scanner

Place the remaining foam piece in the box on top of the scanner. The grooves on the top of the foam piece should be toward the back of the box.

Bag cords and place them in the box

Place the power cord(s) and USB cable in their bags, then place the cord(s) and USB cable around the edges of the box.

Bag the Viewing shield and place it in the box

  1. Wrap the Viewing shield in its bag.

  2. Place the viewing shield on top of the cardboard sheet that has foam around the edges. Ensure the posts on the Viewing shield are placed inside the holes on the cardboard sheet.

  1. Place the cardboard and Viewing shield in the box.

Add the last sheet of cardboard and close the box

  1. Place the remaining sheet of cardboard in the box on top of the Viewing shield.

  2. Shut the flaps.

  3. Securely close the box.

  4. Tape the lid of the box as it was taped when the box was received. Ensure the tape is secure, so the box will not come open during shipment.
