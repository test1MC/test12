Disconnecting the Power

First, exit the by selecting Instrument > Disconnect on the Application menu or by closing the application window. Next, briefly press the power button on the front panel to power off the . To disconnect the power before servicing or moving the instrument, shut down the by pressing the power button on the front panel. Then remove the power cord from the power receptacle as described in Electrical Considerations on page 1.

Note: Do not unplug the power cable or push ‘O’ on the rear power switch while the system is running, except for an emergency situation.
