Obtaining Technical Support

To resolve a problem with your , start by contacting Technical Support at 800-645-4260 (U.S. only), or emailing . Outside of the U.S., contact your local sales office or distributor. Be prepared to give the serial number of your instrument, which can be found on the manufacturer label on the rear panel of the instrument (Figure 1‑1). You can also find the serial number of the instrument in by hovering over the Status group on the Acquire ribbon.

LI‑COR Technical Support may ask you to provide copies of the instrument log files to aid in diagnosing problems.
