Cleaning the Scanning Surface

The scanning surface is plate glass and can be cleaned with any non-abrasive glass cleaner. Warm, soapy, distilled water or isopropanol can be also be used. Do not use scouring compounds or abrasive scouring pads; the glass can scratch, which can affect the scanned image.

It is very important that the glass and silicone mat be free of smudges, dust, and dye before placing membranes or gels onto the . Contaminated surfaces in contact with the membrane surface may cause blotches and streaks that cannot be removed with further washing.
