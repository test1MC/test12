Obtaining Technical Support

To resolve a problem with your Odyssey Fc Imager, start by contacting LI‑COR Technical Support at 800-645-4260 (U.S. only), or emailing biohelp@licor.com. Outside of the U.S., contact your local sales office or distributor. Be prepared to give the serial number of your instrument, which can be found on a label near the computer module on the right side of the instrument.

LI‑COR Technical Support may ask you to provide copies of the instrument log files to aid in diagnosing problems.
