Align Channels

displays a window where the placement of one channel can be adjusted in reference to the other channel. A new image is created when the button is pressed.

This action cannot be undone once the button is clicked.

  1. Select the image that has channels that you want to align.

  2. Click the on the . The is displayed.

  3. Select the Reference Channel from the drop-down menu. This channel will not move.
  4. Use the up or down arrows to move the other channel(s) to the top or bottom, or use the bars on either side of the arrows to move the other channel(s) left or right.
  5. Click . A new image is created with the channels aligned.
