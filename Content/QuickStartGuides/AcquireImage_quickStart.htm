Acquire an Image

  1. Place a sample on the scan bed.
  2.  
  3. Click the Acquire tab (Figure 5).
  4. In the Setup group, select the Membrane Scan Preset from the Scan Preset drop- down menu. Select No Analysis.
  5. Enable the check boxes beside the desired channel(s) (700, 800, or both), indicating the channel(s) selected for image acquisition.
  6. In the Channels group, adjust the settings; 5 is the default setting. If a higher value is selected, the overall image will be brighter; however, if the intensity is set too high, saturation may occur. Saturation is seen as a color change on the image.
  7. In the Scan Area group, select No Image from the drop-down menu of the first button. Select Draw New to drag a new rectangle onto the scan grid and erase any previously defined scan areas. Click the scan grid at the upper left point of the new rectangle and drag to the lower right to define the scan area.
  8. Click Start to start the scan.
